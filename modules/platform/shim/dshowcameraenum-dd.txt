= DShowCameraEnum

== Description

I am a Windows-specific DirectShow camera enumeration shim.
I enumerate DirectShow video capture devices that may not be visible to Qt Multimedia's `QMediaDevices::videoInputs()` on Windows.

I collaborate with `LocalDeviceCatalogue`, which may merge my results with Qt's device list to present a more complete camera list to the user.
I do not capture video frames, I do not implement a device refresh UI, and I do not become a general-purpose DirectShow wrapper.

State model:
- Thread affinity: caller thread.
- Lifetime/ownership: stateless.
- Re-entrancy: re-entrant.

== Public interface

- `QVector<VideoInput> dshowcameraenum::enumerateVideoInputs( QString *outDebugText )` returns the current DirectShow video input snapshot.
  - Behaviour:
    - Windows: best-effort enumeration via `CLSID_VideoInputDeviceCategory`.
    - Non-Windows: returns an empty list.
  - Errors:
    - No explicit failure reporting; may return an empty list.
    - When provided, `outDebugText` contains a short diagnostic message.

- `QString dshowcameraenum::stableIdFromMonikerDisplayName( const QString &monikerDisplayName )` produces a stable vcstream device id.
- `bool dshowcameraenum::stableIdToMonikerDisplayName( const QString &stableId, QString *outMonikerDisplayName )` decodes the stable id.

Implementation note:
- Stable ids are encoded as `dshow:<hex>` where `<hex>` is the UTF-8 hex encoding of the moniker display name.

== Non-goals

- I do not provide DirectShow capture support.
- I do not attempt to determine a default camera.
- I do not deduplicate devices across different backends; callers decide merge policy.
