 = VcVideoPaintItem

== Description

I am VcVideoPaintItem.

I provide a software-renderer-friendly camera/video preview item for the Qt Quick UI.
I collaborate with Qt Multimedia's QVideoSink to receive frames, and with authored QML pages (for example the camera preview panels) to present a reliable preview when the Qt Quick software scene graph adaptation is active.

I do not attempt to replace Qt Multimedia's VideoOutput in the normal hardware-accelerated renderer path.
I do not provide high-performance video playback; I prioritise predictable behaviour and basic frame visibility.

== Motivation

- When `QT_QUICK_BACKEND=software` is active, Qt Quick uses the software scene graph adaptation.
- On Windows with Qt 6.11, Qt Multimedia's `VideoOutput` may accept frames on its `videoSink` but fail to render them, producing a blank preview.
- VCStream uses this item as a fallback preview renderer in that mode to avoid a silent blank panel.

== State model

- Thread affinity: GUI thread only (Qt Quick item).
- Lifetime: owned by the QML object tree; `videoSink` is owned by the item.
- Re-entrancy: not re-entrant.

== Public interface

- `QVideoSink *videoSink` (constant)
  - The sink that receives frames.
  - Frames are throttled for repaint to avoid repainting at unbounded cadence.
- `bool hasFrame` (read-only)
  - True once at least one frame has been successfully converted for drawing.
- `int framesReceived` (read-only)
  - Counts frames observed by the sink.
- `QString errorText` (read-only)
  - Set when frames cannot be converted to an image for drawing.

== Requirements

- Language: C++17.
- Qt: 6.x (QtQuick, QtMultimedia).
